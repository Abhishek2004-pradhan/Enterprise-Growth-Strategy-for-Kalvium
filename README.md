# Enterprise Growth Strategy for Kalvium
### A Competitive Benchmarking Study of the Indian EdTech Ecosystem

## Overview

This project presents a strategic benchmarking analysis of the Indian EdTech ecosystem with a primary focus on **Kalvium** and its major competitors, including **Scaler School of Technology**, **Newton School of Technology**, and **Masai School**.

The objective is to understand how leading organizations differentiate themselves through curriculum design, industry partnerships, student outcomes, branding, and enterprise engagement, and to develop actionable strategic recommendations for Kalvium.

The project is structured as a consulting-style business strategy report inspired by industry benchmarking and Founder's Office research practices.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze the Indian EdTech industry and its evolution.
- Benchmark leading industry-integrated education providers.
- Compare business models, curriculum, partnerships, and value propositions.
- Identify strategic gaps and growth opportunities.
- Recommend enterprise engagement and outreach strategies for Kalvium.
